Supra SAE India

SolidWorksFEAPowertrain B.Tech Major ProjectAug 2018 – Jul 2019

As part of Supra SAE India 2019, our team designed and built a formula-style race car to SAE standards. My focus was the powertrain system.

Design & manufacturing of powertrain components

I designed, analysed and fabricated powertrain components including engine mounts and transmission sprockets. I also developed detailed CAD models for 40+ components across subsystems and integrated them into the full vehicle assembly in SolidWorks — emphasising weight reduction, manufacturability and vibration stability.

Component CAD coming soon

Vehicle integration & structural analysis

The powertrain components were integrated into a tube-frame chassis. FEA and dynamic simulations ensured high-stress regions were reinforced while keeping overall mass optimised for performance. The final design met safety and performance criteria, ready for manufacturing and on-track evaluation.

Chassis model coming soon

Cost analysis & competition performance

I prepared a detailed cost-analysis report covering materials, machining and manufacturing feasibility, benchmarking each part against SAE India's budget criteria. The vehicle was successfully presented and competed at the national event — demonstrating robust design, precision manufacturing and teamwork.
