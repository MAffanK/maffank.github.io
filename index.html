About me

Turning physics into hardware

On the podium at the symposium.

I'm Affan Khan, a mechanical engineer with a Master's from IIT Guwahati. My work sits at the intersection of classical engineering and computation — using CFD, FEA and increasingly machine learning to design things that actually get built.

I like problems where a good model and a real prototype have to agree.

Presenting our bionic arm — fitting it on my guide.

From competition teams to a patent

Along the way I've led powertrain design on a Supra SAE formula car and handled fuselage and aerodynamics for an SAE Aero Design UAV. I also co-developed a 3D-printed compliant bionic arm — presented at a biomedical symposium and resulting in a filed patent.
