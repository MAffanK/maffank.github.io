About me

From CAD & CFD to AI-driven design

On the podium at a symposium.

I'm Affan Khan, a computational-mechanics engineer (M.Tech, IIT Guwahati). I cut my teeth deep in CAD, CFD and FEA — on formula cars, aircraft and lab hardware — and I'm now focused on bringing machine learning into engineering design and simulation.

My north star: AI tools that don't replace physics, but design with it.

Presenting our bionic arm to my guide.

Hands-on across many domains

I've led powertrain design on a Supra SAE car, engineered the fuselage and CFD for an SAE Aero aircraft (All-India Rank 27), and built a 3D-printed compliant bionic arm. In industry and national labs I've worked on underwater robotics, in-space 3D printing and a precision Coriolis flow meter — and I hold a granted patent for a cable-actuated 3D-printer arm.

Presenting at an ISRO symposium.
